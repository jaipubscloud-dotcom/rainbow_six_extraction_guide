Beginner Setup Guide for Lion — Tom Clancy’s Rainbow Six® Extraction
Lion is one of the most beginner‑friendly operators in Extraction, combining balanced stats with a powerful recon ability that gives your squad real‑time enemy tracking. His EE‑ONE‑D drone scan reveals moving Archæans through walls, letting you control engagements before they happen.

🦁 Step 1: Understand Lion’s Role
Lion is a Recon/Intel Support Operator. Your job isn’t to rush enemies — it’s to provide information so your team can plan safe routes and ambushes.

Armor / Speed: 2 / 2 — balanced for mobility and survivability.
Primary Function: Detect moving enemies and stabilize chaotic fights.
Ideal Missions: Rescue, Specimen Capture, and Nest Destruction — all benefit from early intel. 
1

Slot	Recommended Gear	Reason

🧠 Step 3: Master the EE‑ONE‑D Drone
Activation Timing: Use the scan before breaching or when enemies start swarming.
Cooldown Management: Don’t spam — save it for critical moments.
Team Coordination: Announce scans so teammates can reposition or reload safely.
Synergy: Pair with Finka for boosted survivability or Pulse for extra intel overlap. 
3
Pro Tip: Trigger the drone right after noise alerts (like breaking walls or firing loud weapons). Enemies that move will be instantly marked, giving your squad a tactical edge.

🧩 Step 4: Tactical Playstyle
Scout First, Engage Second: Always scan before entering new rooms.
Control Chaos: When fights get messy, use the drone to freeze enemy movement and regain control.
Preserve Operators: If a teammate goes MIA, Lion’s intel helps locate and rescue them safely.
Adapt to Objectives: In multi‑zone incursions, scan early in each sub‑zone to map threats efficiently.
🚀 Step 5: Progression and Upgrades
As Lion levels up, his scan duration and armor improve. Focus upgrades on:

Drone Cooldown Reduction — more frequent scans.
Scan Range Expansion — wider detection radius.
Weapon Stability — smoother control for sustained fights. 
4
⚠️ Step 6: Common Mistakes to Avoid
Overusing the Scan: Wastes cooldown and alerts enemies unnecessarily.
Ignoring Stealth: Lion’s scan can trigger Archæan reactions — coordinate timing.
Solo Rushing: His strength lies in team synergy, not lone combat.
🎯 Quick Practice Drill
Try this in a low‑risk incursion:

Enter a zone quietly.
Trigger EE‑ONE‑D once enemies are heard.
Mark and eliminate detected targets.
Extract early to preserve health.
Repeat until you can time scans instinctively — that’s the foundation of a great Lion main.

With this setup, Lion becomes your squad’s eyes and stabilizer, turning unpredictable incursions into controlled operations. Once you’re comfortable, experiment with deeper zone pushes and combine scans with other recon tools for maximum efficiency.
