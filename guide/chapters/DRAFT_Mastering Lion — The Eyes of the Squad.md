🧠 3. Mastering the EE‑ONE‑D Drone

Lion’s drone is your most important tool and the cornerstone of his tactical advantage. Many beginners underestimate the power of timing and coordination when deploying the EE‑ONE‑D, often leading to wasted scans or missed opportunities. This section will guide you through the nuances of mastering this essential device, ensuring you maximize its potential every time.

Activation Timing

Use before breaching a new room to reveal enemy positions and prevent ambushes.

Use when noise alerts trigger (such as breaking walls or loud gunfire) to capitalize on enemy reactions.

Use when enemies begin swarming to regain control of chaotic fights and disrupt enemy advances.

Use strategically during objective phases to track moving targets and secure mission-critical intel.

Cooldown Discipline

The EE‑ONE‑D has a significant cooldown period, so it’s crucial not to waste scans. Avoid spamming the drone; instead, save it for moments when enemy movement or positioning changes the fight’s dynamics. Effective cooldown management means you’ll always have the intel when it matters most.

Team Coordination

Communication is key to leveraging Lion’s drone effectively. Always call out your scan status clearly to synchronize team movements:

“Scanning now” → signals teammates to reposition safely.

“Scan up — push” → coordinates a synchronized breach or attack.

“Scan up — reload” → provides a safe window for teammates to reload without risk.

“Scan complete — hold position” → instructs the team to maintain their current stance based on intel.

Operator Synergy

Lion’s drone works best when paired with operators who can capitalize on the intel:

Finka: Boosts survivability during aggressive pushes immediately after a scan, allowing the team to capitalize on revealed enemy positions.

Pulse: Combines heartbeat sensor intel with Lion’s movement detection for comprehensive threat awareness.

Thorn: Uses her proximity mines to trap enemies revealed by Lion’s scans.

Alibi: Can bait enemies into revealed positions, making them easier targets.

Pro Tip: Trigger EE‑ONE‑D right after you make noise. Any Archæan reacting to the sound will move — and be instantly marked, giving your team a critical edge in tracking and eliminating threats.

Mastering the EE‑ONE‑D drone is about precision, timing, and teamwork. With practice, you’ll turn this tool into a game-changing asset that controls the flow of every incursion.

🧩 4. Tactical Playstyle Breakdown

Scout First, Engage Second

Lion should never enter a room blind. The sequence is clear: scan first, identify threats, then engage with precision and confidence. This disciplined approach minimizes surprises and maximizes your squad’s survival chances.

Pre-Entry Scanning: Always activate the EE-ONE-D drone before breaching any door or entering new areas. This reveals enemy positions and movement patterns, allowing you to plan your attack or avoidance strategy.

Patience is Power: Resist the urge to rush. Waiting for the perfect moment to scan and strike can turn the tide of battle.

Control Chaos

In the heat of battle, when enemy swarms and frantic firefights erupt, Lion’s drone becomes your anchor. Use it to freeze enemy movement temporarily, disrupting their coordination and giving your team a critical breather.

Freeze Enemy Movement: Activating the scan halts all moving Archæans, exposing their locations and preventing flanking maneuvers.

Re-establish Control: Use the intel to reposition your team, set up crossfires, or retreat to safer ground.

Preserve Operators

When a teammate goes missing in action (MIA), panic can set in. Lion’s scan is your lifeline to locate them safely and identify nearby threats.

Locate Missing Teammates: Use the drone to scan the area around the last known position of the MIA operator.

Safe Recovery: Mark enemies and clear paths to facilitate a safe rescue or regroup.

Multi-Zone Strategy

Each sub-zone presents unique challenges. Establish a routine to maintain control and reduce risk:

Enter Quietly: Avoid unnecessary noise to prevent alerting enemies prematurely.

Trigger a Scan: Use the EE-ONE-D drone immediately upon entry to reveal enemy patrols and nests.

Map Patrols and Nests: Take mental or communicated notes of enemy positions and movement patterns.

Plan Your Route: Chart a safe and efficient path through the zone, avoiding ambushes and maximizing tactical advantage.

This disciplined habit alone prevents approximately 80% of beginner deaths, turning cautious exploration into confident progression.
