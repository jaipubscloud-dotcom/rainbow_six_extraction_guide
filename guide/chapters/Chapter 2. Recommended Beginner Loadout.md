⚙️ 2. Recommended Beginner Loadout

Primary Weapons

Weapon

Why It’s Good

V308 Assault Rifle

Low recoil, clean sight picture, excellent mid-range control. Perfect for beginners who want reliable accuracy and versatility in most combat scenarios.

SG-CQB Shotgun

High burst damage for close-quarters panic moments. Ideal for tight corridors and sudden enemy encounters where stopping power is crucial.

F2 Assault Rifle

Balanced fire rate and damage with manageable recoil, offering flexibility for both mid and close-range engagements.

Secondary Weapons

P9 Pistol — Fast handling, reliable backup when reloading mid-fight, with decent stopping power.

BEARING 9 SMG — Compact and quick, excellent for close-range defense and finishing off weakened enemies.

Gadgets

Claymore — Protects extraction points, choke points, and rescue corridors by preventing enemy flanks and surprise attacks.

Recon Drone — Extends your intel network beyond Lion’s scan, allowing for additional scouting and situational awareness.

REACT Tech

Scan Grenade — Stacks beautifully with EE-ONE-D for layered detection, revealing enemies hiding behind cover or in hard-to-see spots.

This loadout balances offensive capability with defensive utility, ensuring beginners can adapt to various mission types while maximizing Lion’s unique intel-gathering strengths.
