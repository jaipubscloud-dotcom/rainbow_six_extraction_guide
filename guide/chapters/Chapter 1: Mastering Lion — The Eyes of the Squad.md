🦁 Understanding Lion’s Battlefield Role

Lion is not a rusher — he is the eyes of the squad. His mission is to reveal threats, control pacing, and prevent ambushes.

Operator Profile

Role: Recon / Intel Support

Armor / Speed: 2 / 2 (balanced mobility and survivability)

Primary Function: Detect moving Archæans, stabilize chaotic fights, and guide safe routes

Best Mission Types:

Rescue — scan to locate hostiles around the VIP

Specimen Capture — track the target’s movement

Lion is strongest when he controls information flow. You win fights before they begin.
